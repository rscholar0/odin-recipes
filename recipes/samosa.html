Samosa Recipe

Description

The picture above depicts Samosas, a traditional South Asian cuisine dated to Nepal and India. It is delicious and filled with potatoes, herbs, and more ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Species
    • Ajwain seeds
    • Amchur
    • Asafoetida
    • Black mustard seeds
    • Garam Masala
    • Cumen seeds and power
    • Coriander seeds
    • Turmeric
  • Flour
  • Green Chilli
  • Ghee or oil
  • Potatos
  • Ginger
  • Peas
  • Coriander/cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes then mash it roughly until it is done
  2. Cook spices with health dose of fresh ginger and chilli with peas
  3. Add the mashed potates and mix with the cooked species
  4. Add fresh coriander then let it cool
  5. To make the dough, first mix the flour, Ajwain seeds and salt, then pour the ghee or oil in
  6. Form a dough and let it rest for 30 minutes
  7. Shape the dough into balls and roll them out to 2 mm thick sheet
  8. Cut the sheet in half and brush the sides with water
  9. Form a cone and place some potato filing we made earlier inside the cone
  10. Seal the cone and pinch top of conee to make it pointy and nice
  11. Now fry the sealed pastry for 3 minutes at 160°C/320°F
  12. Let the oil drain on the paper towel then fry again until golden
  13. Lastly, drain the oil on paper toweel and you are done!
